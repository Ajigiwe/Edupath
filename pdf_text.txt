Information & Admission Deficit
Every year, thousands of Ghanaian SHS graduates face 
intense rejection from universities like UG and KNUST 
simply because they lack clear, structured information 
regarding required aggregate grades and specific cut -off 
points.Academic & Resource Scarcity

to learn ahead or construct clear career plans.The transition problem in Ghana
